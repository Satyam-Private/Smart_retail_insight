About Smart Retail Insights

Welcome to Smart Retail Insights, a platform designed to help retailers analyze and visualize their sales data efficiently. Our tool allows you to upload CSV files containing supermarket data, such as product details, sales, and customer information, to gain valuable insights into your business operations.

With Smart Retail Insights, you can:

  1. Analyze sales trends and patterns.
  2. Segment customers based on purchasing behavior.
  3. Optimize inventory management.
  4. Make data-driven decisions to grow your business.

This platform is developed by Satyam Patil & Chinmay Kulkarni, with the goal of providing retailers with an easy-to-use tool for data analysis. Whether you're a small business or a large supermarket chain, Smart Retail Insights is here to help you succeed.
